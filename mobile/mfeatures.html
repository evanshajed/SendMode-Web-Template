Time Critical Alerts

SMS is the ideal tool for time-critical alerting applications. It can be targeted to a single user and, by using tyntec's high-quality messaging services, can be guaranteed to arrive in a timely fashion.

From alerting investors about stock price movements to warning homeowners of dangerous weather conditions, SMS alerting can enable organisations to quickly reach their audiences wherever they are at any time of day in urgent situations - a unique capability in a messaging system.

Updates And Remnders

Updates and reminders via SMS can provide real consumer value and can help organisations build closer relationships with their customers. From a reminder of a medical appointment to an update on a bank account balance, SMS delivery can provide personalised and timely information to end users quickly and efficiently.
